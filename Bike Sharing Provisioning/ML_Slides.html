Recommendations (from a technical perspective)¶

  1. Execute two separate models as different factors influence registered users and casual users to bike in Washington D.C.
  2. More information to improve the model: registration costs, usage of other modes of transport in the city, location of bike stations, traffic and frequency of usage on the same day.

Summary¶

  1. The most preferred feeling temperature of users is between 31 and 35 degrees Celsius to use the bike service.
  2. Most users use the bike services around 8 in the morning and around 5-6 in the afternoon. This indicates a higher uptake of the service during rush hours.
  3. The service is used consistently over all days of the week, i.e. there is no preference to bike only during the weekdays or only during the weekends.
  4. Usage definitely decreases as the weather becomes "less clear"
  5. More granular data with more information will help improve the predictive power of the model.
